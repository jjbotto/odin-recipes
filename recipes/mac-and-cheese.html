Mac n' cheese

Description: Creamy and cheesy mac and cheese packed with an assortment of cheeses and baked with a crispy breading top

  • Box of favorite type of pasta
  • Bacon
  • Chedder cheese
  • Mozzerrella cheese
  • Colby Jack cheese
  • Milk
  • Bread crumbs
  1. Boil pot of water on stove
  2. Add noodles and reduce heat until cooked
  3. Strain noodles
  4. Add cheese and cooked bacon back into pan with pasta
  5. Add breadcrumbs on top and place in over for 5 minutes
