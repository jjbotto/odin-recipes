Overnight Oats

Description: These Overnight Oats are loaded with protein and offer an quick and easy meal that is healthy and tastes great!

  • Old-Fashioned Oats
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Protein Powder
  • Chia Seeds
  • Milk
  • Honey
  1. Combine the oats, protein powder, and chia seeds in a container such as a mason jar or tupperware
  2. Add in the milk, Greek yogurt, and honey. Mix until well combined, making sure that no chunks of protein powder are stuck to the bottom
  3. Cover the lid and place in the fridge overnight
  4. Add in any extra ingrediants as desired, such as fruit and peanut butter
