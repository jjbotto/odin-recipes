Laura's Stromboli

Description: Laura's Famous Stromboli is an easy to make dish that is completely customizable with anyone's desired toppings and is the perfect food to serve at an event or gathering!

  • One package of preferred dough
  • Deli-style ham
  • Deli-style pepperoni
  • Provolone cheese
  • Ground sausage
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Unroll dough to make flat on cutting board
  3. Layer cheese, ham, pepperoni, and sausage
  4. Roll dough up with toppings inside
  5. Place in oven for 20 minutes
  6. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes before eating
